The Balloon Keepy Uppy Society

The Balloon Keepy Uppy Society at MTU ofers a wide range of training for all skill levels. Both to those who are new to the sport and those who are hardened veterns, we have something for everyone. We compete at many competitions and intervarsities and have a prestigious coaching team. While the sport of Balloon Keepy Uppy has been active for many years it is only recently it has gotten the attenion it deserves. The rules are simple keep the balloon in the air, by any means necessary. So come join us for your chance at glory and greatness.
